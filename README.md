## üìà Insights & Analysis

### 1. Revenue Analysis
- **By Card Category:** The **Blue** card category contributes the highest revenue ($9.2M), followed by Silver, Gold, and Platinum.
- **By Usage Type:** "Swipe" transactions generate the most revenue ($7.0M), significantly outperforming Chip and Online transactions.
- **By State:** Texas (TX), California (CA), and New York (NY) are the top revenue-generating states.

### 2. Customer Demographics
- **Education Level:** Graduates contribute the highest share of revenue and credit usage, followed by High School graduates.
- **Marital Status:** Married individuals account for the majority of the spending amount (approx. 50.5%), compared to single individuals (42%).
- **Gender Distribution:** The spending split is relatively balanced, with one gender contributing ~55.62% ($25M) and the other ~44.38% ($20M).

### 3. Financial Metrics
- Analysis includes a breakdown of **Interest Earned** vs. **Spending Amount** across different education levels.
- A dual-axis analysis tracks the correlation between **Revenue** and **Credit Limits** based on education level.
